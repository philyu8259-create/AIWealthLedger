## Description

```text
AI Wealth Ledger helps you capture spending faster and keep your personal finances organized.

Whether you type an expense, scan a receipt, or review your recent spending, the app is designed to make personal bookkeeping simpler and clearer.

Key features:

• Quick expense entry
Add income and expenses with a clean, lightweight bookkeeping flow.

• AI-assisted transaction parsing
Turn natural language input into structured bookkeeping records.

• Receipt OCR scanning
Scan receipt images and extract useful text for faster entry.

• Spending insights and prediction
Review recent patterns, warnings, and AI-generated spending analysis.

• Asset and stock position tracking
Track personal assets and supported stock positions in one place.

• Guest mode and sign-in options
Use the app in guest mode, or sign in with supported account methods depending on your device and build.

• VIP subscription support
Unlock premium features through Apple In-App Purchase.

Notes:

• Feature availability may vary depending on device capability, sign-in state, network status, provider availability, and current build configuration.
• AI, OCR, and market data features rely on third-party services.
• The app is intended for personal bookkeeping and productivity, not for professional financial, tax, or investment advice.
```

## App Review Notes

```text
This submission is for the international iOS build of AI Wealth Ledger.

Core functions in this build:
1. Manual bookkeeping
2. AI-assisted text parsing for transaction entry
3. Receipt OCR scanning
4. Spending analysis / prediction
5. Asset and supported stock-position tracking
6. Guest mode, Google sign in, and Sign in with Apple
7. VIP subscription via Apple In-App Purchase

Permissions used:
- Camera / Photo Library: used for receipt image selection and OCR
- Microphone / Speech Recognition: used when the user chooses voice-based input on supported devices

Third-party services used in this build:
- Google Vision for receipt OCR
- Gemini for AI parsing and spending insights
- Finnhub for supported US stock search and quote data
```
